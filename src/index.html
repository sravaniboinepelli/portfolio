Hello there!

I'm Sravani Boinepelli, currently based in Austin, Texas. I have experience in Generative AI, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and full-stack development.

I graduated with a B.Tech. (Honors) and an M.S. by Research from the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-H), one of Asia's premier institutes for Computer Science.

At IIIT-H, I contributed to impactful research at the Information Retrieval and Extraction Lab (iREL), focusing on the intersection of AI, Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing. I collaborated with Prof. Vasudeva Varma (IIIT-H) and Niyati Chhaya (Adobe Research) on Project Angel, which was recognized with the Google India AI/ML Research Award.

I'm always open to meaningful conversations and collaborative ventures. If you'd like to connect, exchange ideas, or explore potential projects, don't hesitate to reach out.
